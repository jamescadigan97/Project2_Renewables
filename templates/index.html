Renewable Energy Production in the US

In this project, we hope to demonstrate were renewable energy is being produced in the United States and how much energy is being produced.

The production of renewable energy is diversified across the United States. The California, Texas, Arizona and other states in southwestern United States produce the most solar energy. Hydroelectric power production is largely concerntrated northwestern states like Oregon, Washington, Idaho and parts of California. Wind energy production is greatest in mid-western states and Texas.

The sunburst chart at the end shows that renewable energy comprises of about 20% of the energy production in the US in 2019. Nearly 40% of that renewable energy production is wind power. Hydro-electric is the next most common form of renewable energy, followed by solar power. Those three forms of energy production account for about 90% of all renewable energy production.
